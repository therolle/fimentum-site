# Fimentum Brand Strategy

## Positioning Statement

**"Your funding co-pilot for growth"**

Fimentum is the approachable, experienced partner who navigates the complex Finnish/EU funding landscape alongside founders - not above them.

---

## Brand Pillars

### 1. Track Record
- €200M+ raised for clients
- 1000+ successful projects
- 25+ years of experience

### 2. Insider Knowledge
- Deep expertise in Business Finland
- EU/Horizon program specialists
- Finnvera, ELY connections
- VC and angel network

### 3. Founder DNA
- Tommi Pajala: serial entrepreneur
- Founded 20+ companies
- Peer-level credibility with founders
- Coach at Kasvu Open

### 4. No-BS Approach
- Results over jargon
- Hands-on involvement
- Cuts through bureaucracy
- Direct, practical advice

---

## Tone of Voice

### Do
- Be direct and clear
- Use plain language
- Sound confident but approachable
- Speak as an equal partner
- Focus on outcomes and results

### Don't
- Use consultant jargon
- Sound superior or academic
- Overpromise or hype
- Be vague or evasive
- Talk down to founders

### Example Copy

**Instead of:**
> "We leverage our extensive network and strategic methodologies to optimize your funding acquisition journey."

**Write:**
> "We help you find the right funding and get it. No complexity, just results."

---

## Key Differentiators

1. **Entrepreneur, not consultant** - Tommi has built and funded his own companies
2. **Hands-on partner** - Involved throughout, not just strategy decks
3. **Connected ecosystem** - Kasvu Open, Business Tampere, EU networks
4. **Full spectrum** - Both startup and growth company expertise
5. **Local + international** - Finnish funding landscape + EU programs

---

## Target Audiences

### Primary: Finnish Startups (Seed to Series A)
**Pain points:**
- Don't know where to start with Business Finland
- Overwhelmed by funding options
- Time-consuming application processes

**Needs:**
- Guidance on which funding to pursue
- Help with applications
- Strategic advice on timing

**CTA:** Book initial consultation

### Secondary: Growth Companies (Series A+)
**Pain points:**
- Scaling requires complex capital mix
- EU programs seem inaccessible
- Limited internal resources for funding

**Needs:**
- Strategic funding roadmap
- EU program expertise
- Ongoing funding management

**CTA:** Book strategy session
